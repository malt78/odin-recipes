Salmon Fillets

Description:

Fire up succulent blackened salmon with an exciting blend of Cajun-style spices!

Ingredients

  • Ground paprika
  • Ground cayeene pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Mix all seasonings in together in a small bowl.
  2. Brush both sides of the salmon fillet with 1/4 cup of butter. Sprinkle with mixed seasonings.
  3. Heat a skillet with high heat, add salmon and cook until blackened. Flip salmon and repeat.
