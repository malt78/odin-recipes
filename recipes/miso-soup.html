Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup that starts with a dashi stock and is flavored with miso paste. This is a Jamie favorite.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi granules
  • Miso paste
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Bring the water and dashi granules to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in tofu and green onions.
  4. Simmer and serve.
