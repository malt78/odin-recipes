Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

How to make bakery-worthy, soft, chewy, and absolutely irresistible chocolate chip cookies. This is a Jason favorite.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking soda
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix together the butter, egg, sugar.
  2. Disslove the baking soda in hot water and add the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Place the dough on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees until edges are golden brown.
